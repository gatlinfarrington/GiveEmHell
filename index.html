Mission Statement

The Give 'Em Hell Movement is a grass-roots membership based group built on creating great, high energy experiences for our members and other Sun Devil Fans. The movement is centered around the following pillars.

Attendance

Butts in seats. Period.

Atmosphere

Creating an incredible home field advantage for our teams

Activity

Support our teams however you can. Fly a flag on Game Day, post on social media, show off your Sun Devil fandom how you see fit.

Advocate

Bring Sun Devil fans together to have a greater voice in ways to improve overall game day experience.

FUN

Our events will be engaging, high energy, and fan centered

Who Are We?

Simply put, we're a group of Sun Devils who want to make the Game Day Experience a better time for ourselves and others.
